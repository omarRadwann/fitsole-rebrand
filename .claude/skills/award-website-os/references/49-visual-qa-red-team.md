# 49 — Visual QA Red Team

After screenshots exist, critique the site like an award jury and a skeptical buyer.

Reject the work when:

- the hero has no memorable image or idea
- the hierarchy is unclear in three seconds
- the mobile version loses the concept
- motion feels decorative
- 3D looks random or harms performance
- proof feels fake or vague
- the CTA is hidden
- spacing looks accidental

Record findings in `docs/visual-review.md` and fixes in `docs/qa-report.md`.
