## Brutal Questions

1. Would a visitor understand the offer in five seconds?
2. Does the hero look like this specific business or a template?
3. Is there one memorable visual idea?
4. Does typography feel intentionally directed?
5. Are sections rhythmically different or just stacked blocks?
6. Does motion reveal meaning or merely decorate?
7. Does 3D explain, prove, or create memory?
8. Does mobile feel designed, not compressed?
9. Is the CTA obvious and low-friction?
10. Are all proof claims real or truth-labeled?

## Fail Conditions

- One screenshot cannot be used as a brand artifact.
- Hero headline could fit any business.
- CTA is vague.
- 3D is a random object.
- Mobile loses the concept.
- Accessibility is weakened by visual effects.
- Asset rights are unclear.
