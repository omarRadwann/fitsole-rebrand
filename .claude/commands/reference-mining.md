# /reference-mining

See `.claude/skills/reference-mining/SKILL.md` for the full protocol.

## Step 1 — confirm scope

Open `docs/one-input-brief.md`. Confirm category, geography, and project mode. Mining is category-specific; don't mine globally.

## Step 2 — search the standard sources

- Awwwards SOTD / SOTY / HM, last 18 months.
- FWA, CSSDA, SiteInspire, Httpster, Land-book.
- 2–3 named studio portfolios in the category.

Record search queries and candidate URLs in `docs/reference-mining-guide.md`.

## Step 3 — curate the board

In `docs/benchmark-reference-board.md`, produce exactly:
- 3–5 in-category references.
- 3–5 out-of-category references.
- 3–5 anti-references (the category clichés).
- Max 3 specialist references (typography, motion/3D, copy).

For each, name what to steal (technique) and what to reject. References without both fields are discarded.

## Step 4 — synthesize

Three sentences at the bottom of `benchmark-reference-board.md`:
1. What this category currently looks like.
2. What it could look like.
3. Where this project sits between those two.

## Step 5 — mining test

Of all curated references, which would the user be most upset to see Awwwards link to ours as "reminded me of"? If "any of them", push the concept further.

Hand off to creative-director with the curated board.
