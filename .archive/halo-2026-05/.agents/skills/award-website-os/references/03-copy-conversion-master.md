# 03 — Copy and Conversion Master

## Mission

Make the site clear, persuasive, and credible.

## 5-Second Test

Above the fold must answer:
1. What is this?
2. Who is it for?
3. Why should I care?
4. What do I do next?

## Hero Formulas

### Outcome-first
`[Achieve valuable outcome] without [painful tradeoff].`

### Pain-to-control
`Turn [painful chaos] into [controlled outcome].`

### Category creation
`The [category] for [audience] who [urgent need].`

### Trust-led
`[Specific service] delivered with [proof/standard] for [audience].`

### Luxury/emotion-led
`A calmer way to [desired transformation].`

## Weak Words to Avoid

Avoid:
- seamless
- innovative
- next-generation
- revolutionizing
- unlock potential
- cutting-edge
- world-class without proof
- tailored solutions
- robust platform
- empower your business

Replace with:
- specific pain
- specific user
- specific mechanism
- specific outcome
- specific proof

## Proof Without Fake Proof

If no proof:
- show process
- show sample deliverable
- show founder story
- show guarantee
- show methodology
- show transparent FAQ
- show mock demo clearly labeled as demo
- show "what you can expect"

Never:
- fake logos
- fake reviews
- fake user count
- fake awards
- fake before/after

## CTA Rules

CTA must match intent:
- Book a consultation
- Request a quote
- View selected work
- Start assessment
- Schedule a visit
- See the system
- Build my plan
- WhatsApp us

Avoid generic "Learn more" as primary CTA.
