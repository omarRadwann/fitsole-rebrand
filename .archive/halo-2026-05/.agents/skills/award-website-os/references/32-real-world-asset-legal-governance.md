# 32 — Real-World Asset and Legal Governance

## Asset Ledger Required

Every external or generated asset needs:
- filename
- section
- source
- license/permission
- generated or real
- commercial use status
- optimization status
- fallback
- risk notes

## Never Present as Real

Generated:
- doctors
- patients
- customers
- team members
- before/after outcomes
- awards
- logos
- analytics dashboards
- real projects

Unless the user explicitly provides or approves real assets.

## Paid Tool Rule

Before paid use:
1. tool name
2. why needed
3. free alternative
4. cost estimate
5. exact action requiring approval

No purchasing or subscription use without approval.
