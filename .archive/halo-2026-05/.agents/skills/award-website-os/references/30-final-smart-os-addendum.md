# 30 — Final Smart OS Addendum

This is the final top-level instruction layer.

## The Master Standard

The site should feel like it was made by:
- a strategist who understands the business
- a creative director with taste
- an art director with restraint
- a copywriter who knows conversion
- a frontend engineer who respects semantics
- a motion designer who knows when to stop
- a 3D/WebGL expert who protects performance
- an accessibility specialist
- a QA lead who refuses weak work

## Smartest Rule

Do not maximize features. Maximize:
- clarity
- memorability
- trust
- conversion
- speed
- accessibility
- maintainability

## The One-Line Final Test

```txt
Does this website make the business feel more valuable, more trustworthy, and more memorable without making it harder to use?
```

If no, continue.

## The Strongest AI Instruction

```txt
Do not produce the most likely website.
Produce the strongest website that this specific business deserves.
```

## What To Do When Unsure

- research
- simplify
- make assumptions explicit
- pick a strong direction
- build
- inspect screenshots
- fix obvious issues
- disclose limits

Do not freeze asking endless questions.

## What To Remove

Remove:
- generic copy
- extra cards
- decorative icons
- random motion
- unnecessary 3D
- duplicate sections
- weak CTAs
- fake proof
- heavy assets
- default component styles

## What To Add

Add:
- specificity
- proof
- motif
- hierarchy
- polish
- accessibility
- performance
- mobile intent
- one memorable interaction
