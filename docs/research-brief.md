## Category landscape (B2B-SaaS performance/brand-hybrid agencies)

Three archetypes dominate the category website-wise:

1. **The Gallery.** Big photography, slow scroll, named clients as proof. Awwwards-style. Examples in the wild: Made By Reign, Order, Locomotive (when not building for clients). Failure mode: looks like the work, doesn't reveal the method. Useful for in-bound from creative-led brands, useless for B2B-SaaS founders who need to evaluate methodology in 90 seconds.
2. **The Brochure.** WordPress/Webflow template. Hero says "Powerful marketing for ambitious teams." Below: services grid, logos, testimonial carousel, "book a call." Failure mode: indistinguishable from every other agency. Loses the methodology-sensitive buyer.
3. **The Founder Letter.** Increasing — single-page agencies (e.g. recent Substack-published "studios") with a personal letter, public pricing, public refusals. Examples: Postlight (more dev than marketing but the pattern), small writer-founded brand studios. Strength: builds trust fast with operators. Weakness: indistinguishable from a blog, hard to communicate scale or process.

**Caliper sits at the intersection of #1 and #3.** Visual ambition of The Gallery; transparency, pricing, and methodology of The Founder Letter; rejects #2 entirely.

## Target audience research notes

Founders at $5–50M ARR B2B SaaS:
- Read by *The Generalist*, *Lenny's Newsletter*, *Stratechery*, *Letter & Spirit*
- Hire from networks more than RFPs
- Have been burned at least once by a $20K/mo retainer that produced two reports and a blog post
- Convert on: written proof of opinion (founder letters, case essays), pricing transparency, a defined first-engagement scope
- Do NOT convert on: case-study video reels, "we are storytellers"

## Conversion mechanic

The fixed-fee diagnostic is the differentiating unit of buying. Concretely:

- **Diagnostic** — $9,500 fixed, 2 weeks. Output: one written diagnostic brief (12–20 pages) + a 90-min playback. Includes a public refusal section: what we will NOT touch and why.
- **Build** — $25–80K project fee, scoped from the diagnostic. Output: a shipped artifact (landing page, sequence, pricing page, positioning doc). Three weeks max.
- **Retainer** — 3 months minimum, $18K/mo. Two shipped artifacts per month + a Friday session.

The site must lead with the **Diagnostic** because it's the only safe first commitment. Buyers should be able to learn the entire diagnostic methodology on the site without talking to anyone.
