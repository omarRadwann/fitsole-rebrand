# 02 — Concept and Art Direction Master

## Mission

Create a website world that belongs to this business only.

## Concept Competition

Always generate 3 territories:

### Safe Premium
High trust, commercially strong, refined, low risk.

### Experimental
More memorable, distinctive layout, one unique interaction.

### Moonshot
Bold metaphor, signature interaction, possibly 3D/WebGL/cinematic.

## Concept Scoring

Score:
- Design
- Usability
- Creativity
- Content
- Developer craft
- Business usefulness
- Feasibility
- Performance risk

Minimum target:
- Design: 8.5
- Usability: 8
- Creativity: 8.5
- Content: 8
- Craft: 8

If no concept passes, generate again.

## One-Sentence Concept Formula

`This website presents [business] as [visual metaphor/world] so that [audience] feels [emotion] and understands [core value].`

Examples:
- A construction-tech product as a living blueprint that becomes operational reality as users scroll.
- A luxury dental clinic as a quiet light studio where confidence is restored with precision.
- A logistics platform as a city grid where chaos resolves into clear movement.

## Typography Direction

Choose intentionally:
- editorial serif + neutral sans
- grotesk + mono technical
- high-contrast display + warm readable body
- geometric sans + grid discipline
- variable font with width/weight motion

Rules:
- maximum two type families in most cases
- display type must be art-directed
- body copy must be readable
- use clamp scale
- mobile type must be designed

## Color Direction

Prefer controlled palettes:
- warm off-white + graphite + one accent
- midnight + luminous accent
- mineral neutrals
- medical calm without default blue
- luxury without lazy black/gold
- construction material palette: concrete, steel, safety accent

Use OKLCH/CSS variables where possible.

## Layout Direction

Use:
- editorial asymmetry
- strong grid
- large type + small precise metadata
- section rhythm
- sticky narrative once, not everywhere
- bento only when grouping means something
- proof near CTA
- mobile-specific composition

## Signature Interaction Requirements

A signature interaction must:
- be tied to the business metaphor
- be memorable
- work on mobile
- have reduced-motion fallback
- not hide critical content
- not damage performance

Examples:
- blueprint lines assemble into product dashboard
- 3D object reveals service layers
- scroll turns chaos into clarity
- cursor becomes measuring tool
- image masks reveal before/after responsibly
