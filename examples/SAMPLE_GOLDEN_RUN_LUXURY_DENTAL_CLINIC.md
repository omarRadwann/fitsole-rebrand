# Sample Golden Run — Luxury Dental Clinic

## Input

Business type: luxury dental clinic in Cairo.

## Assumptions

- Audience: affluent adults/families seeking cosmetic and advanced dental care.
- Primary CTA: book consultation / WhatsApp.
- Trust needs: doctor credentials, calm process, hygiene, technology, transparent appointment flow.
- Avoid: fake doctors, fake patients, stock smiles, cold blue template, black/gold cliché.

## Concept Territories

### Safe Premium
Calm editorial clinic experience with ceramic/glass material language.

### Experimental
"Light Studio for Confidence" — soft beams and layered treatment journey.

### Moonshot
A 3D ceramic tooth/mineral sculpture that reveals service layers on scroll.

## Selected

Experimental, with restrained 3D only if performance allows.

## Asset Policy

- No generated patients.
- No fake before/after.
- Use abstract clinic/material visuals.
- Real doctor/team photos required later.
- Generated hero may be abstract only.

## Signature Interaction

Scroll reveals layers of the care journey: consultation, scan, plan, treatment, follow-up.

## Mobile

- Static poster visual.
- Sticky WhatsApp/book CTA.
- Tap service cards.
- No heavy 3D by default.

## Ship Decision Example

PASS WITH LIMITATIONS until real clinic photos, credentials, and reviews are provided.
